# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Please be respectful, constructive, and direct.

We want Lumina to be a project where people can:

- ask basic questions without embarrassment
- give technical feedback without hostility
- disagree on code and design without personal attacks

## Unacceptable Behavior

This includes:

- harassment or intimidation
- hateful or discriminatory language
- personal attacks
- doxxing or sharing private information without permission
- repeated bad-faith disruption of discussions

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, email `nyigoro@gmail.com`.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, lock discussions, or block participation when needed to protect the project and its contributors.
